Description

CoderSpace hosts an 8-week summer program where students learn the basic coding languages that power the web (HTML, CSS and JS). Students gain deep experience in web development, teamwork and leadership as they work together in small teams to design and build websites for local businesses.

Schedule

The CoderSpace summer program runs July 6 - August 28, 2015. Sessions are held Monday - Friday, 10:00am - 4:00pm.

Location

Students attending high school for the 2015-2016 academic year are eligible to apply. Priority will be given to students who live in the following community areas:
